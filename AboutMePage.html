My Career Choice/Plan 🦋

Choosing to pursue a career as a radiology technician has been a clear path for me, rooted in my desire to work in the medical field and contribute to a hospital environment. After exploring various careers, I found that radiology perfectly aligns with my interests and skills. I appreciate the balance of working both independently and as part of a team, allowing me to collaborate with others while also engaging in focused tasks. My experience in design and development, particularly in creating apps and projects aimed at alleviating users' pain, has deepened my empathy and awareness of the challenges people face. This understanding, coupled with the skills I’ve gained from meeting deadlines and working collaboratively, will serve me well in my future career. I intend to attend college to become a licensed technician, specializing in MRI and CT scans. I’m genuinely excited about the opportunity to learn about these technologies and the impact they have on patient care.
